THE QITE MODEL

How QITE Works

A structured three-phase approach designed to prepare you for career success through exploration, training, and real-world experience.

Home How QITE Works

Explore → Train → Intern

Our proven three-phase model ensures you're fully prepared, confident, and ready to contribute from day one of your internship.

1-2 Weeks Exploration 2-4 Weeks Training 8-12 Weeks Internship
Phase 01

Exploration

Discover your strengths, interests, and ideal career path before making any commitments.

  • Career assessments: Identify your strengths, values, and interests
  • Industry deep-dives: Learn from professionals about day-to-day realities
  • Career coaching: 1-on-1 sessions with experienced career coaches
  • Job shadowing: Observe professionals before committing to an internship
Duration: 1-2 weeks
Phase 02

Training

Build the foundational skills you need to succeed before your internship begins.

  • Micro-courses: 20-40 hour courses specific to your chosen field
  • Live workshops: Interactive sessions with industry experts
  • Technical skills: Hands-on practice with tools and technologies
  • Soft skills: Communication, teamwork, and professional etiquette
Duration: 2-4 weeks
Phase 03

Internship

Apply your learning in a real-world setting with dedicated mentorship and support.

  • Real projects: Meaningful work that impacts the business
  • Dedicated mentor: Weekly 1-on-1 guidance and feedback
  • Performance reviews: Regular check-ins to track your growth
  • Networking: Build connections with industry professionals
Duration: 8-12 weeks
